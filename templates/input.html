Which 2020 Presidential Candidate's Music Is Most Like Yours?

The New York Times published an article analyzing the music played at campaign rallies of the 2020 United States Presidential candidates. Using the playlists that they published for each candidate, this app compares your Spotify listening history to each candidate.

Click below to see which presidential candidate's rally music is most like your music!
